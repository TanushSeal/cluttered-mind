Date: 16/01/2026

Problem Statement:

Let n ∈ ℕ. Consider the alphabet {a, b} and define the language L_n to consist of all words in which the difference between the number of occurrences of a and the number of occurrences of b is divisible by n. Show that for all n, L_n is regular.

Solution:

Since \( L \) can be described by the regular expression \[ \Sigma\Sigma\Sigma^* \] the language is regular.
